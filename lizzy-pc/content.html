IDEAS

PROBELM

What happens if people don’t get a good quality of sleep?

It is generally known that people will feel groggy, irritable, and having trouble in focusing ontasks. Therefore, Sleep is an essential factor for humans to survive same as food, water, and oxygen.

According to the research, adult moderate sleep disorders associated with higher levels of fatigue, mood disturbances, can lead to depressive symptoms, lower quality of life. Because of sleep issue, there are so many people out there who struggle with stress, anxiety, insomnia and depression.

SOLUTION

User's body seems to be in a quiet island, mind feel sleepy struck, and eyelids close slowly. Then they could go into the sleep state. On the basis of sleep, user can extend immersive using the scene, it is a small partner when user want to get meditation or inspiration.

KEFEATURE

This application is designed to establish the principles on the basis of ASMR.

ASMR differs from other sleep music, because of a unique sensation to occur in the people’s head that is extremely relaxing, brain feels thrillingly good, and it is often described as a "tingly" reaction to specific sounds, visuals, and cognitive stimuli. These sounds, and stimuli are referred to as “triggers” because they elicit the tingling sensation. The project would use the triggers like slow, specific, and deliberate actions or speech to make people relaxed.

While some people have triggers, or others have audio stimuli that triggers a very negative response to them. Therefore, The application will use triggers testing to find people’s triggers.

When did you first experience ASMR?

The research show most people get ASMR this sensation when there are child. Becuase most people recall this feeling, when people talk slowly, or move slowly, or even sometimes drive slowly.

What does ASMR seem to help people?

The research show that ASMR helps them to feel less stressed and helps them to fall asleep. Most people agree that some special sounds elicit a sensation liket they are being touched on their head or body. therefore, ASMR is a pleasurable, relaxing experience.

Age, Device and Experience ASMR?

The research show that young people need ASMR to help more than other people because they from stress at a much higher rate than generations that preceded them.

The research show that young women are more interested in ASMR than man, because women are twice as difficult as men to fall asleep or stay asleep, and a 10% to 30% stress increased was found through surveys on 30-age young women.

TRIGGERS

This tingling sensation is euphoric, and a sound or a stimuli that make people relaxing.

Here are the most common triggers list, the application will use these triggers as the default sounds.

Touching the head

Touching your head or hair can be a trigger in the real world, however sometimes it can also be a trigger when simulated in a voices.

Whispering

This is possibly the most popular ASMR trigger, at least if the prevalence of You Tube sounds is anything to go by.

Scratching and tapping

This trigger are the strongest, always similar and not enough variety will make people are not used to them, so that become immune. Tapping sounds usually go side by side with scratching sounds as they both are usually done by nails.

Personal attention

The trigger can encompass many different situations generally presented in ASMR voices through different role plays. Common themes include haircuts, facials, doctor’s examinations.

TARGET MARKET

The target market of Immersive are people who is insomnia, or work pressure. They want to use technology, especially voice, as a way to relax. There's an entire universe of sound creation on application designed to make them feel good.
